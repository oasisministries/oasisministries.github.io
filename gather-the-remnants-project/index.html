Gather The Remnants Project

News / By oasisofhope

Empowering tomorrow's generation today.

Partnering to build a world where all teens & youth are empowered to be valued contributors to tomorrow’s society.

The Oasis Ministries recently began a project,… ‘Gather The Remnants’ which is aimed at empowering teens and youth to be productive contributors of tomorrow’s society by taking them through a series of intensive training on subjects such as:

Better Government Relations – Creation of Awareness &
Capacity Building on Government Policies, Better Parent & Society Relations, Time Management, Romantic Relationships Training, Financial/Business Training, Career Path Guidance, Training on Life Philosophies & Moral Code To Live By, Tips For Significance Success, Spiritual Growth Training, Healthy (Drug Abuse Free) Lifestyle, Music plus much more,…

These trainings first commenced in form of a Seminar that lasted from 22nd – Aug – 2018 to 25th – Aug – 2018 and the purpose is to hold these trainings at least twice every year during semester breaks. The trainings may take different forms each time they are held, i.e: Camps, Seminars, Trips e.t.c. The trainings were began in responce to the plight of many parents and the society at large for them to be held as a result of many teens and youth being exposed to many modern social ills that often times results to the youth engaging in drug abuse & radical immorality. The Church, on hearing the plight of these parents, decided to begin the ‘Gather The Remnats’ project.

Our desire is to better the society we live in that has been overrun by drug abuse, radical immorality and crime. Majority of the people engaging in these vices are the teens & youth. We strongly believe that if they can be taken through intensive training with a caring and loving support group , they can be transformed.
